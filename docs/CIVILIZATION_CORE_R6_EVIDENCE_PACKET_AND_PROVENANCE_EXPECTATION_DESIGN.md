## 6. Evidence Packet Definition and Method

A documentary evidence packet is a bounded prose-and-reference bundle assembled for human review; it is not a schema, record, stored object, runtime payload, ingestion unit, validation result, score, verdict, approval, authorization, or execution instruction.

- EPD-01 — Evidence packet language names a bounded documentary bundle for a human reviewer.
- EPD-02 — Its method is descriptive assembly in prose and references, not a machine process.
- EPD-03 — It may describe source observations, references, context, interpretation separation, provenance, time, conflict, gaps, and notes.
- EPD-04 — It excludes schema, record, object, payload, pipeline, and runtime meanings.
- EPD-05 — It relates source and evidence to interpretation without collapsing those distinctions.
- EPD-06 — It keeps provenance, uncertainty, dissent, gaps, and temporal qualification visible.
- EPD-07 — Human Owner review stops when packet language could imply validation, authority, or execution.
- EPD-08 — Handoff is a documentary candidate for human review only.
- EPD-09 — Review order remains a documentary orientation and does not define operational sequence.
- EPD-10 — Instantiation remains outside this prose-only documentary design.

## 7. Provenance Expectation Definition and Method

A provenance expectation is a documentary statement of what origin, attribution, custody, transformation, temporal, version, and contextual information should remain visible to a human reviewer; it is not a provenance validator, cryptographic proof, chain-of-custody engine, completeness guarantee, authenticity verdict, or implementation requirement.

- PED-01 — Provenance expectation language describes visibility a human reviewer should be able to inspect.
- PED-02 — Its method preserves origin and attribution as documentary context.
- PED-03 — It may describe custody, transformation, editorial handling, version, time, and known gaps.
- PED-04 — It excludes validator, proof, engine, guarantee, verdict, and implementation meanings.
- PED-05 — It relates source and reference context to human interpretation without certifying either.
- PED-06 — It keeps uncertain attribution, conflict, missing context, and freshness qualification visible.
- PED-07 — Human Owner review stops if expectation language becomes a compliance or enforcement mechanism.
- PED-08 — Handoff is a non-authoritative description for human consideration.
- PED-09 — Automatic provenance checking remains outside this prose design.
- PED-10 — Provenance expectation order is documentary only.

## 17. Documentary Sufficiency and Non-Verdict Boundary

Documentary sufficiency is qualitative, scope-bound, human-observed, and non-automatic. It is not truth, correctness, completeness certification, validation, approval, authorization, adoption, implementation readiness, or production readiness.

No arrow is automatic.

- DSN-01 — Documentary presence means that a described source or reference is visibly represented for review.
