## 16. Documentary Human Decision Points

Each point names the accountable human role or Human Owner, required documentary context, a possible documentary outcome, an explicit non-effect, no automatic next step, and no runtime behavior. Not every candidate must pass through every point.

- HDP-01: The Human Owner confirms Human Owner and review responsibility from named-scope context; the outcome may record responsibility, but it creates no account, authority, next step, or runtime behavior.
- HDP-02: The accountable reviewer confirms the review question and scope from sources, exclusions, and purpose; the outcome may record bounded scope, but it creates no expanded scope, transition, or runtime behavior.
- HDP-03: The Human Owner decides whether evidence is sufficient to continue human review from evidence, provenance, uncertainty, conflicts, dissent, and gaps; the outcome may be continue, hold, defer, or stop, but it proves no truth and triggers nothing.
- HDP-04: The accountable reviewer chooses a documentary review disposition from rationale and unresolved concerns; the outcome may record continue, revise, request evidence, defer, reject, or close, but it creates no proposal or runtime transition.
- HDP-05: The Human Owner decides whether to begin a separately scoped proposal discussion from the completed review record; the outcome may request a new human scope, but it neither creates nor accepts a proposal and triggers nothing.
- HDP-06: The intended human requester decides whether to formulate a separate approval request from bounded evidence and rationale; the outcome may be formulate, defer, or decline, but it issues and routes no request.
- HDP-07: An accountable approving human authority makes a separate approval decision from a separately authored request and its basis; the outcome may be bounded, conditional, deferred, rejected, or withheld, but it authorizes and executes nothing automatically.
- HDP-08: An accountable authorizing human authority makes a separate authorization decision from exact subject, scope, target, purpose, limits, and safety context; the outcome may record a bounded decision, but it adopts, mutates, and executes nothing.
- HDP-09: An accountable human adoption authority makes a separate adoption decision from authorized scope and documentary basis; the outcome may adopt, defer, reject, or withhold, but it performs no mutation or execution.
- HDP-10: An accountable human mutation authority makes a separate mutation decision from adopted scope, impact, reversibility, and safety context; the outcome may authorize separate consideration, but it performs no write or execution.
- HDP-11: An accountable human execution authority makes a separate execution decision from exact action, actor, target, scope, limits, and safety context; the outcome may authorize or withhold a separately governed action, but this artifact performs no action.
- HDP-12: The Human Owner may defer, stop, or close documentary consideration from the preserved record; the outcome is documentary only and creates no deletion, escalation, follow-on work, or runtime behavior.

No state machine, workflow, sequence engine, approval chain, or decision router is created.
